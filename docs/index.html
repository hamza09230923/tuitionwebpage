GCSE Tuition That Helps Students Close Gaps, Build Confidence and Improve Grades

MySchola provides online small-group Maths, English and Science tuition for Year 9, Year 10 and Year 11 students. Lessons are built around GCSE exam technique, a dependable weekly structure, homework, feedback and clear parent updates. How MySchola Works

We begin with a free consultation to understand your child's current level, exam board and weak topics. Students then join weekly small-group lessons and track progress through homework, tutor feedback and parent updates.

GCSE Maths, English and Science Tuition

Our GCSE Maths tuition supports Foundation and Higher students with topic gaps, problem solving and exam questions. GCSE English tuition develops reading, writing, analysis and essay technique. GCSE Science tuition covers Biology, Chemistry and Physics for Combined and Triple Science, with clear explanations and relevant exam practice.

Year 9, Year 10 and Year 11 Tuition

Year 9 tuition builds strong foundations early. Year 10 tuition closes gaps before mocks and creates a consistent revision routine. Year 11 tuition focuses on intensive exam technique, targeted revision and past-paper preparation.

Built for Busy Parents Who Want Clear Progress

Parents receive clear communication, homework tracking, progress updates, WhatsApp support and monthly Zoom progress calls. Many students have improved by two or more grades after consistent lessons, homework and exam practice, although every student's starting point and outcome is different.
